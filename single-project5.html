EXCHANGR IDENTITY

PeiRong's sacrifice

One night, PeiRong's suspicions about XiaoWei are confirmed when she chances upon XiaoWei peeling off her human skin right in front of her and revealing her true form. Peirong and XiaoWei come to an agreement: XiaoWei promises to stop killing people; in return, PeiRong offers her place as Wang Sheng's rightful spouse to XiaoWei and takes the blame for the murders. PeiRong says" You don't understand love at all." Then XiaoWei gives PeiRong a potion to drink, after which PeiRong's hair turns white and her features become "demonic".

The city's residents are horrified when they see Peirong and think that she is the demon. Just as PeiRong is about to be killed by the citizens, PangYong and XiaBing show up, save her, and bring her to a cave.
