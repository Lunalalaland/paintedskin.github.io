The woman you love doesn't love you.

One night, when XiaoYi meets XiaoWei undercover, he warns XiaoWei that love between demons and humans is impossible and pleading her to leave with him instead of continuing the stupid crush on WangSheng. However, XiaoWei couldn't take in any word of him, she believes in herself strongly enough that WangSheng will fall in love with her.
